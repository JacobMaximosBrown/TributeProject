Donovan Mitchell, an NBA Superstar on, and off of the court

The Prelude

In 2017 a team within the NBA (National Basketball Organization) known as the Utah Jazz finished their regular season winning a surprising amount of 51 games led by a young Duo of Rudy Gobert and Gordon Hayward, but that duo wouldn’t last long. During the 2017 Playoffs the Utah Jazz were able to overcome odds and upset the favored Los Angeles Clippers, led by Chris Paul. The Jazz were able to make it to the second round but were easily dispatched by the eventual champion Golden State Warriors. The Off-season was very important to the Utah Jazz, due to it being their All Star Gordon Haywards free agency, and that resulted in him leaving Utah for the Boston Celtics. The effect of Gordon Hayward leaving Utah for nothing was supposed to leave Utah as one of the worst teams in the league, however on rookie player drafted surprisingly embraced the challenge, and beat all odds. His name is Donovan Mitchell.

The start of something great

Donovan Mitchell didn’t start right out of the gate as the “savior” the Utah Jazz needed, but he showed from day one he had the potential to do so. Donovan Mitchell gradually increased his level of play until he was named the starter for Utah in december of 2017, where he took off from and never looked back. Donovan Mitchell was in the run for the Rookie of the year, which he would lose to Ben Simmons due to his slow start. Donovan took all of the negativity from losing the Rookie Of The Year and proved that he was the better player than his rival by pulling up one of the biggest upsets in basketball history by beating the Oklahoma City THunder led by Superstars Russell Westbrook, Paul George, and former All Star Carmelo Anthony. Donovan Mitchell's rookie year was just the start of his fantastic career on and off of the court.

What he has done within the NBA

Donovan Mitchell has achieved a plethora of achievements and awards already in his young career. He has been selected three times as an NBA All Star, has won 3 player of the month awards, was on the 2017-2018 All-Rookie First Team, and won the 2018 NBA Dunk Contest. Donovan has won awards throughout the regular season, however his best play comes within the playoffs. Donovan is the 7th All Time highest PPG Scorer in NBA History. Donovan Mitchell is a truly remarkable player and will be for a very long time.

What he has done outside of the NBA

Donovan Mitchell has done a whole lot on the court, but he has done more off of it. Donovan Mitchell won the NBA's NBA cares award for the 2020-2021 season due to his many contributions to society. The three main societal contributions from Donovan Mitchell are; His fight for Civil Rights particularly in 2020, his fight against social & economic injustices, and his giving back to the community through his SpidaCares organization. Regardless of your political stances it is a certainty that Donovan Mitchell has improved the Salt Lake Community, The New York Community, and now the Cleveland Community.

Why I admire Donovan Mitchell

Donovan Mitchell is my favorite player in the NBA for many reasons, his explosive play, his amazing skill, but my favorite thing about him is his willingness to accept any challenge to fight for what he believes in despite the odds. Donovan proved this many times, and even though my favorite team traded him to the Cleveland Cavaliers I will always admire and respect him.
